App Name: Smile Shine

+ This is an educational app, based on having proper dental care.
+ In this app, 6 preventions are discussed for smiling shine.
+ In the home screen, while the app is launched, 6 images will be seen along with a button beneath it.
+ These are the buttons to show the details of 6 preventions: Brushing, Flossing, Wiping, Rinsing, Health and Check-Up

- After pressing "Brushing"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its importance
	+ Later on, how-to/ technique will be seen on regular 2 times brushing

- After pressing "Flossing"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its importance
	+ Later on, how-to/ technique will be seen on flossing everyday

- After pressing "Wiping"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its fruitful decription

- After pressing "Rinsing"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its importance
	+ Later on, how-to/ technique will be seen on salt water mounth rinsing/ any other material available in the market

- After pressing "Health"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its friutful description

- After pressing "Check-Up"
	+ An image along with some text fields will be seen in order to provide its friutful description

N.B. After going to the another activity, home screen can be viewed by just tabbing onto back from any Android device
